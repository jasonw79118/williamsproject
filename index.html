Contributor guide

Best evidence: original records, census images, marriage records, probate, deeds, military records, cemetery records, Bible pages, and DNA segment details.

Privacy: living people should be hidden or reviewed before public display. A static GitHub Pages site cannot truly protect private facts once they are committed to the repository.

Research status: unproven Williams parentage, Sarah Williams/Lee, Samuel Williams, and older Williams chains stay as research leads until supported by records or DNA.
